Welcome to MeetPoint

MeetPoint is a space for socializing, refreshing, and showcasing opportunities from different sites and users. Discover jobs, education resources, promotions, and more— shared by the community, for the community.

Connect

Meet and interact with people who share your interests and goals.

Discover

Find jobs, scholarships, trainings, and opportunities curated by users.

Share

Post insights, promotions, images, or videos to inspire and educate others.

How to Get Started

1. Register & Login

Create your account by clicking "Login" in the top navigation, then "New here? Register". Fill in your details and start connecting with the community.

2. Upload Profile Picture

Click on the circular profile area in the Events section to upload your profile picture. This helps others recognize you in the community.

3. Browse Categories

Select from four main categories: Education, Jobs & Careers, Promotions & Business, or Community & Social to view relevant posts.

4. Create Posts

Click "New Post" to share opportunities. Select a main category, add optional sub-category and tags. Include social media links in tags (e.g., "LinkedIn: your-profile") for private contact.

5. Stay Connected

You'll receive notifications when others post in your selected category. Your posts appear on the right, others' posts on the left for easy identification.

6. Share Feedback

Help us improve MeetPoint by sharing your thoughts, suggestions, and ideas through the Feedback section. Your input helps us enhance the platform for everyone.

💡 Pro Tips

  • Social Media Sharing: Add your social media links in the tags field (e.g., "Instagram: @username, LinkedIn: profile-url")
  • Category Selection: Choose the most relevant category for better visibility
  • Sub-categories: Use specific sub-categories like "Scholarships", "Remote Jobs", "Local Events"
  • Tags: Use descriptive tags to help others find your content
  • Media: Attach images or videos to make your posts more engaging
  • Feedback: Use the Feedback section to suggest improvements, report issues, or share ideas for new features
